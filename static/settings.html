Step 1 — Companies House API Key

Free. Gives you company search, director names, and ownership details.

1

Go to developer.company-information.service.gov.uk

Register with your Appear Here email, then click "Create an application".

2

Select Live environment. Name it anything (e.g. "Property Owner Finder").

Your API key will appear — copy it.

3

Give the API key to whoever deployed this app. They need to add it as an environment variable called COMPANIES_HOUSE_API_KEY in the hosting dashboard (e.g. Railway → Variables).

Step 2 — Land Registry Data (CCOD & OCOD)

Free. Lists every UK property owned by a company. This is the big one.

1

Go to use-land-property-data.service.gov.uk

Sign in (create a free account with your Appear Here email).

2

Download these two datasets:

• "UK companies that own property in England and Wales" (CCOD) — ~1GB file
• "Overseas companies that own property in England and Wales" (OCOD) — smaller file

3

Upload them here (one at a time). The CCOD file is large so it may take a minute.

Step 3 (Optional) — Land Registry Business Gateway

For automated title searches on individually-owned properties. £3 per search. Most teams won't need this.

1

Apply at gov.uk/guidance/hm-land-registry-business-gateway

Approval takes 2-4 weeks. Once approved, add LR_BUSINESS_GATEWAY_USER and LR_BUSINESS_GATEWAY_PASS as environment variables in your hosting dashboard.
